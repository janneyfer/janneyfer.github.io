PICC placement

Supplies

Steps

Identify the cephalic or basilic vein

  1. Position the patient's arm in abduction
  2. Using ultrasound identify the cephalic vein along the lateral upper arm or the basilic vein along the medial arm
  3. Make sure the vein is compressible

Prepping the patient

  1. Place mask and bonnet on patient
  2. Place chuck under the patient
  3. Clean with chlorhexidine.
    4. Clean a second time with chloroprep.
  4. Gown up and put on sterile gloves
  5. Fold the top two inches of a sterile drape towards you and carefully tuck it under the patient
  6. Clean the skin twice with chloroprep
  7. Drape the neck with 3 sterile blue towels. Open up a sterile blue towel and fold to form a "W" and place above target vein covering the face. Place blue towels on the left and right of the vein forming a triangle
  8. Remove the adhesive and place the hole over the vein. Open up the drape to cover the patient's head and body
